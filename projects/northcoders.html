What

This was a group task where we had less than 2 weeks to come up with an idea and present the idea at graduation. Unfortunately we did not have time to deploy the app.

Ramble is a group trip organising App, which incorporates a chat bot to build your itinerary for you. You can talk to the chatbot to add key elements to your trip such as dates, location. There is a function to add your friends to a trip and a group chat where you can chat with your friends on that trip. You can have multiple trips.

My main focus was on the front end, so working with React-Native, creating API calls, incorporating the group chat function and chat bot.

  • Pair programming.
  • Learning new tech in a short space of time.
Tech

React Native, Dialogflow, gifted messenger, firebase, Node.js, Express, PostGreSQL.

2 weeks with 5 of us in our team.
