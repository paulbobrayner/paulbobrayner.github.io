About Paul

Skills

React, Angular, Node.js, C#, SQL.

Why coding?

I had been working in the health industry for around 8 years and had been toying with the idea of software development for a few years before joining northcoders. My main reason for moving on from health coaching was that I wanted a new type of challenge. Coding had been on my radar as I had set my own wordpress site up for my nutrition coaching business and dabbled very mildy in html, css and dns.

After a few online coding courses I signed up to Northcoders for a December 2018 start. Northcoders was a 12 week coding bootcamp focused on full stack Javascript. I can safely say that Northcoders was one of the hardest things I have ever done. Mostly due to the amount of content delivered and the intensity of the course.

Receiving my first job offer two weeks after the end of the course proved to me the power of a well delivered course and trusting in the process.

Moving into coding has been one of the best decisions I have made. I love the problem solving, the chance to create and the continual learning.

My ambition

To delve deeper into full stack development, continue to challenge myself and build new and more complex software.
